Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

How long to get started?
Usually 48 hours. But honestly, it depends on you. Give us your scripts and info today, we could be live tomorrow. The longest part is often making the decision — and we get that.
Is there a minimum commitment?
Nope, zero. You can stop anytime with 7 days notice. Why? Because we know if it doesn't work out, you'll leave anyway. Might as well be upfront about it.
How does billing work?
Simple. Outbound: you pay for hours actually used. Inbound: monthly package. AI agents: per minute. No hidden fees, no surprises on the invoice.
What accents do you offer?
US (standard and regional), Canadian, British, Australian. For AI, it's your choice. For human agents, we match someone who fits your clientele.
Can I switch plans?
Yes, anytime. Up or down. Billing adjusts pro-rata. If you grow, we grow with you. If you slow down, we adapt.
Is my data secure?
Hosting in Canada (and EU for European clients). End-to-end encryption. GDPR compliant. We don't sell anything, don't share anything. Your data = your data.
