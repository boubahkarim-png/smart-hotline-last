Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started

How long to get started?
Setup takes 48h maximum after signing. We configure your scripts, train agents and test together before launch.
Is there a minimum commitment?
No. All our plans are commitment-free. You can modify or cancel anytime with 7 days notice.
How are calls billed?
Outbound calls: billed per hour actually used. Inbound calls: monthly package including call volume. AI agents: billed per minute.
What languages do you support?
Native English (US, Canada) and French. Our AI agents support both languages with regional accents.
Can I change plans during my subscription?
Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade anytime. Billing is adjusted pro-rata.
Is my data secure?
100% GDPR compliant. Hosting in Canada/EU. End-to-end encryption. No data sold or shared.
