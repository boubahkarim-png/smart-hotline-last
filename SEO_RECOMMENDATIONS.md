## 📹 VIDEO TRANSCRIPT SUMMARY

### Key Strategy from Video: "Stealing Competitor Traffic"

The video teaches a 5-step systematic approach:

1. **Analyze Competitor Traffic** (SimilarWeb)
   - Check total visits (example: 416,000 monthly)
   - Identify traffic sources: Direct, Organic, Paid, Referral, Social
   - Analyze geographic distribution
   - Determine mobile vs desktop split

2. **Find Keyword Gaps** (SEMrush/6Explorers)
   - Shared keywords (competitors rank for)
   - Exclusive keywords (only you rank for)
   - Example: 1,230 shared vs 84 exclusive

3. **Target Low Competition Keywords**
   - Filter by competition ≤ 0.3
   - Look for 100-500 searches/month
   - Quick wins with lower effort

4. **Use AI for Content Creation**
   - Google AI Studio for SEO briefs
   - Claude for content writing (best for SEO)
   - Include tables, lists, FAQ sections

5. **Check Backlink Requirements**
   - Use backlink checker tools
   - Determine how many backlinks needed
   - Compare vs competitor profiles
