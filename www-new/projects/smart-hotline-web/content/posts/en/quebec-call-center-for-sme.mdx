### Why a Quebec Call Center, And Not Something Else?

Let's be real here - offshore call centers can be complicated. The agents don't know the Quebec market, they use European expressions that sound off to local ears, and your customers? They notice.

A local call center is different. Our agents understand that when someone mentions their "char," they're talking about their car. They know that "magasiner" means going shopping. These little things? They make a huge difference in customer relationships.

### Quebec SMEs Are Our Specialty

### How It Actually Works

Pretty straightforward, actually. We set up a phone number just for you. When a call comes in, our receptionists answer in your company's name. They follow a script we prepare together, based on what you need.

After the call? You get a message via SMS, email, or directly in your CRM. Your choice.
