# Outbound Calling

## Overview
Smart Hotline's outbound calling service handles lead generation, appointment scheduling, customer follow-ups, and survey campaigns. Professional callers represent your brand with custom scripts and real-time CRM integration.

## Capabilities
- B2B and B2C lead generation campaigns
- Appointment scheduling and confirmation calls
- Customer satisfaction surveys
- Payment reminders and collection calls
- Event invitation and RSVP management
- Real-time reporting and call analytics
- CRM integration (Salesforce, HubSpot, custom)
- Quality monitoring and call recording

## Pricing
From $18-22 CAD/hour per agent. Campaign packages from $499 CAD/month. Custom pricing for high-volume campaigns.

## Availability
Business hours configurable per campaign timezone. Montreal, QC, Canada headquarters.
