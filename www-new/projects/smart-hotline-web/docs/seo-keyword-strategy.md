**Keywords:** "centre d'appels quebec", "call center services", "externalisation centre d'appels"

**User Expectation:** 
- Pricing information
- Service packages
- Contact form/phone
- Trust signals (testimonials, certifications)

- Clear pricing page
- Prominent CTAs
- Free trial offers
- Client testimonials

**Keywords:** "agents ia vocaux", "externalisation pme", "call center outsourcing"

**User Expectation:**

- Case studies
