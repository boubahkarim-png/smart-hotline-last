# Inbound Call Reception

## Overview
Smart Hotline provides professional 24/7 inbound call reception services for SMBs. Every call is answered live — by human telephonists during business hours and by Sophie, the AI voice agent, after hours. Zero missed calls guaranteed.

## Capabilities
- Live phone answering 24/7/365
- Custom scripts branded to each client's business
- Intelligent call routing and transfers
- Message taking with immediate notification via email/SMS
- Bilingual reception: French (Quebec, France, Belgium, Switzerland) and English
- AI agent Sophie handles after-hours calls with natural conversation
- CRM integration for automatic call logging

## Pricing
From $15-19 CAD/hour (trial rate). Monthly packages from $299 CAD. Volume discounts available for 100+ calls/month.

## Availability
24/7/365 — Montreal, QC, Canada. Serving clients across Canada, France, Belgium, Switzerland, and the United States.
